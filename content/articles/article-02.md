# Don't Eat the Yellow Snow

A song by Frank Zappa.

This is the first part of the "_Don't Eat the Yellow Snow suite_", which opens the album "**Apostrophe**". The suite follows an eskimo, Nanook, inside a dreamlike setting which gets more distorted the more the suite goes on.

Released in 1974 as a single, this was the first Zappa song to hit the charts, at #86, although it only constitutes the first 45 seconds of the single version, with “Nanook Rubs It” comprising of the rest of the run time.
